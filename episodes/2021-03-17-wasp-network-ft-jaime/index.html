Wasp Network ft. Jaime

We sit down with our good friend Jaime and discuss the 2019 Olivier Assayas film Wasp Network

Wasp Network is a 2019 film based on the true story of a series of Cuban defectors and the Cuban exile community in Miami. We’re joined by our good friend Jaime to disucss the movie, and Jaime helps fill us in on Cuba, and Miami Cubans.

Hosted by Liam, Russian Sam, Halal Sam, Abram, & Jaime.
